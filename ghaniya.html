My favourite movies are Blade Runner 2049, Bullet Train, The Nolan verse (if you know, you know), and La Vita e bella. My favourite movie character is Officer K from Blade Runner. My favourite composer besides the legends Ennio Morricone and Hans Zimmer would be Brian Tyler. My favourite community still and always be meme community.
